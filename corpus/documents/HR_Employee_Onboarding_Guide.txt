# Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Meridian Corp. This guide outlines the EHRC cycle onboarding process for all new hires across the five operational divisions: HR, IT, Finance, Legal, and Operations. Each new employee is assigned an onboarding cohort identifier, ranging from COH-001 to COH-400, and must complete 47 discrete tasks within their first 90 days of employment.

The onboarding journey begins with the DASH portal registration, where employees receive their V-SEC tier classification and TRITON system credentials. Within the first 14 days, all hires must complete the LEXA foundational training module, which covers Meridian Corp's core values, the 14-day SLA commitment structure, and the ASCEND performance tracking methodology. Employees assigned to the Delta Project will receive specialized access credentials after completing the general onboarding track.

Department-specific orientation sessions are scheduled during weeks 2 and 3. IT personnel undergo additional ZULU gateway certification, while Finance hires complete the NEXUS expense platform tutorial. All new employees must shadow a veteran colleague for a minimum of 16 hours during the first month. The Q-SCORE evaluation system will assess onboarding progress at the 30-day, 60-day, and 90-day marks.

Meridian Corp provides a welcome package valued at $625.00, including branded equipment, a security token for the TRITON system, and an EHRC cycle handbook. Benefits enrollment must be completed within 14 days of the hire date to ensure continuity. The MOR-2026-045 mandate requires that all onboarding documentation be stored in the centralized repository for a minimum of 7 years. Questions regarding the onboarding process should be directed to the HR Service Center.

Meridian Corp also provides a digital welcome portal accessible through the TRITON system, containing interactive modules on corporate culture and the 14-day SLA framework. New hires receive personalized onboarding checklists generated by the NEXUS system based on their role and department. The ZETA onboarding tracker ensures no step is overlooked during the critical first 90 days.
